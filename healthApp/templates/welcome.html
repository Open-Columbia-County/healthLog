Welcome to the Mental Well-Being Health Log

This application is intended to help you keep track of your mental health. Everyday we are bombarded by different things. Being able to keep track of them and find patterns is essential to your health.

In this app you will find a universal bank of symptoms that have been created by all users that you can add to your daily log.

Please feel free to log in and join in keeping track of your health

The site is still being worked on and being improved. Any changes made will not change or remove your logs
