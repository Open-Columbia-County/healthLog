Everyday we are bombarded by different things. Good and bad. Keeping track of your health can help you find patterns and can help you and medical professionals better understand what is going on.

This application is intended to help you keep track of your health. You will find a universal bank of symptoms that you can add to your logs. Medications you add are also added to a universal list (no connection to you) We are constantly improving and adding new features to this site. Any changes made will not change or remove your logs
